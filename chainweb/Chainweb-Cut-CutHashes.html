CutHashes

data CutHashes Source #

This data structure is used to inform other components and chainweb nodes about new cuts along with some properties of the cut.

Generally, the Cut is represented by providing the BlockHash of the respective BlockHeader for each chain in the Cut.

Optionally, a node may attach the PayloadData and/or the BlockHeader for some of the block of the Cut.
